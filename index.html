DISCOVER OUR JOURNEY

The ITS Marine Solar Boat Team (ITS MSBT) from the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology is focused on building solar-powered vessels. The team is currently completing a vessel called the Jalapatih VI boat, featuring active hydrofoil innovation.

ACHIEVEMENTS

1st
JALAPATIH I

First ASEAN solar boat presented in Global Competition (Dong Solar Energy Challenge 2014).

2nd
JALAPATIH II

2nd Runner Up in Asia Dutch Solar Challenge.

3rd
JALAPATIH III

3rd Position in Top Speed Category Solar Sport One.

OUR LEGACY

JALAPATIH V

Jalapatih V is lighter. The use of carbon material with the vacuum infusion method makes the ship 50% lighter than its predecessor. Jalapatih V used a planing hull that can help the hydrofoil in providing an angle of attack.

JALAPATIH IV

Jalapatih IV was the first hydrofoil solar boat in Indonesia. Hydrofoil technology increased the boat's speed and efficiency. Jalapatih IV is the first boat to use hydrofoil technology in Indonesia.

JALAPATIH III

Jalapatih III had a longer endurance and it used a flexible solar panel to reduce the boat’s weight. Jalapatih III can be land. It was equipped with electrical instruments that sent the boat’s technical information. Jalapatih III won as 3rd Top Speed Category on Solar Sport One.

JALAPATIH II

Jalapatih II was designed to improvthe boat’s stability. Jalapatih II use carbon fiber as its main material. Therefore, it provided a strong but light body. Jalapatih II won 2nd Runner Up in Asia Dutch Solar Challenge 2016.

JALAPATIH I

Jalapatih I was designed to have a dynamic boat hull and good stability as a fin stabiliser attached to the body. Jalapatih I competed in The Dong Solar Energy Challenge 2014.
